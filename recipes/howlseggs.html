Howl's Eggs

ハウルの朝ごはん

Ingredients

3 x slices thick cut bacon

4 x large eggs

1 x loaf bread

1 x slab cheese

1 x large egg

Instructions

Heat a large pan over medium-high heat

Add 3 slices of bacon to the pan and cook until crispy.

Push them to one side of the pan.

Reduce the heat to medium.

Crack 4 eggs directly into the pan next to the bacon.

Cook until sunny side up eggs are to your preference.

Remove the pan from the heat.

Cut desired amount off from the loaf of bread.

Serve the bacon, eggs, bread, and cheese family-style.
