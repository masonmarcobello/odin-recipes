Laputa's Toast

ラピュタ トースト

Ingredients

1 x loaf of Japanese milk bread (bread) or an uncut loaf of thick white bread

1 x large egg

Instructions

Cut the bread about 1 x inch thick

Toast the bread

Fry your egg sunny side up

Place your egg on the toast

Enjoy!
