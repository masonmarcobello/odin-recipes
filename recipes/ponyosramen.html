Ponyo's Ramen

ポニョのラーメン

Ingredients

1 x pack of instant pork or chicken noodles

1 x round, thin gammon steak

1 x egg

2 x spring onions

Boiling water

Instructions

Prepare the noodles as the packets instructs you to do.

Fry the gammon and cut into two halves.

Boil the egg and cut it in half.

Chop up the spring onions.

Lay the two pieces of gammon on top of the noodles.

Also put in half the egg and a spoon full of the onions.

Wait until it cools otherwise it might be hot! hot! hot!

Have fun with Sosuke!
